About Malaysia Travel

Introduction

Our website is called Malaysia Travel. Our website mainly introduces and promotes tourist attractions and foods in Malaysia to tourists.


Our Mission

This website have travel package for tourists who visit our website as a reference and also can contact us for further information.To market Malaysia as a destination of excellence and to make tourism a major contributor to the social-economic development of the country.


Our Objectives

  • Showcasing the unique wonders, sights and culture of Malaysia.
  • Increase tourism revenue in Malaysia by increasing the number of tourists visiting Malaysia and extending their stay.
  • Promote Malaysia as an outstanding tourist destination.
