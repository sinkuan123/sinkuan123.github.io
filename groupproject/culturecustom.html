Culture Customs in Malaysia

Malay

The Malays make up the largest ethnic group and tend to practise both Islamic and Malay traditions and speak the native Malay language.

Chinese

Chinese culture is dominant and covers a large geographical region in eastern Asia, with customs and traditions varying greatly between provinces, cities, and even towns as well.

Indian

Today, the Indian community accounts for about 7% of Malaysia’s population and is considered the third largest single ethnic group in Malaysia.
