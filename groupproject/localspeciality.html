Local Speciality in Malaysia

Top Johor Local Speciality

Pepes

Pepes are fish, seafood or chicken wrapped in banana leaves and cooked. The meat is mixed with many spices and can be grilled, steamed or boiled.

Top Kedah Local Speciality

Madu Kelulut

It is pristine and wild, produced by stingless bees that breed in remote corners of the Kedah rainforest away from pollution and urbanization.

Top Melaka Local Speciality

Pineapple Tart

The fragrance of butter, milk, and egg yolk is greasy, and the aroma of pineapple is used to make delicious pastries, which have become a specialty of Malacca.

Top Negeri Sembilan Local Speciality

Seremban Siew Pow

A crispy baked pastry bread with meat fillings, best served warm while the pastry is still crunchy, light and crispy.

Top Terengganu Local Speciality

Keropok Lekor

This Terengganu food is made from fish that is ground and shaped like a sausage. Keropok lekor are often served with sweet chilli sauce.
